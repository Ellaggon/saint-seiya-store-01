# PAGES_STRUCTURE

## Public Storefront

- `/` (Home): Featured Saint Seiya releases and latest news.
- `/catalog`: Grid with character/line filters.
- `/product/:id`: Technical specs, character lore, and "Add to Cart".

## Customer Area

- `/account/orders`: History and shipping tracking.
- `/account/settings`: Profile and shipping addresses.

## Admin Sanctuary (Dashboard)

- `/admin/inventory`: Stock and warehouse locations.
- `/admin/products`: CRUD for figures, categories, and characters.
- `/admin/orders`: Shipping management and status updates.
